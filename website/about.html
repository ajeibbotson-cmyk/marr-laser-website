About Us

Our Story

Built on a Passion for Skin Health

Marr Laser & Skin Clinic was founded with a clear mission: to bring clinical-grade skin and laser treatments to Newry and the surrounding area. From the very beginning, we set out to combine genuine medical expertise with a warm, welcoming environment where every client feels heard, informed and at ease.

What started as a small practice driven by one practitioner's dedication to skin health has grown into a trusted destination for clients across Northern Ireland. That growth has never changed our core belief — that exceptional results come from understanding each person's skin, building a relationship of trust, and providing treatments grounded in science and clinical evidence.
