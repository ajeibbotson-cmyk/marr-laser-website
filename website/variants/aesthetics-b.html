Common Questions

Aesthetics FAQ

Is Botox safe?

Yes. Botulinum toxin has been used safely in medical and aesthetic settings for over 30 years. At MARR, all anti-wrinkle injections are administered by qualified prescribing practitioners who understand facial anatomy in detail. Side effects are typically mild and temporary, such as minor bruising or redness at the injection site.

How long do dermal fillers last?

Results typically last between 6 and 18 months depending on the area treated, the type of filler used and individual metabolism. Lip fillers generally last 6 to 9 months, while cheek and jawline fillers can last up to 18 months. We will discuss expected longevity during your consultation.

What is the recovery time?

Most injectable treatments require minimal downtime. You may experience slight swelling, redness or bruising for 24 to 48 hours. Anti-wrinkle injections typically show no visible signs within hours. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to help you achieve the best possible results.

What is Profhilo and how does it differ from fillers?

Profhilo is a bio-remodelling treatment, not a filler. Rather than adding volume to specific areas, it disperses across the skin to stimulate collagen and elastin production. The result is an overall improvement in skin quality, hydration and firmness. It is ideal for those who want better skin rather than structural changes.

How do I know which treatment is right for me?

The best way to find out is through a consultation with our practitioners. We will assess your skin, discuss your goals and recommend a tailored treatment plan. There is no pressure to proceed, and we are always honest about what will and will not work for your individual needs.

Can I combine treatments?

Absolutely. Many clients benefit from combining treatments for a more comprehensive result. For example, anti-wrinkle injections and dermal fillers work well together, as do Profhilo and skin boosters. Your practitioner will advise on the best combination and timing for your treatment plan.
