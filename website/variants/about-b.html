The Journey

How MARR Began

From nursing roots to Newry's most trusted laser clinic.

2014 — The Beginning

A Nursing Foundation

Emma Marr began her career as a registered nurse, developing a deep understanding of patient care, clinical safety and skin biology. It was during these early years that her passion for aesthetic medicine took shape, setting the foundation for everything that would follow.

2016 — First Clinic

Opening in Newry

With specialist training in aesthetic medicine and laser therapy complete, Emma opened MARR Laser & Skin Clinic in Newry, Co. Down. From day one, the focus was clear: evidence-based treatments delivered with genuine care.

2019 — Alexandrite Investment

Gold-Standard Laser Technology

MARR invested in the Candela GentleLase Pro, the alexandrite laser widely regarded as the gold standard for hair removal. This commitment to the best available technology set the clinic apart from competitors relying on IPL or diode alternatives.

2022 — Expanding the Team

Growing the Practice

As demand grew, Emma carefully expanded the team, bringing in qualified skin therapists and an aesthetic doctor. Each team member is trained to the same exacting standards, ensuring a consistent experience for every client.

2024 — Where We Are Now

A Trusted Destination

Today, MARR is one of the most trusted skin and laser clinics in Northern Ireland. Clients travel from across the region for our specialist treatments, drawn by word-of-mouth recommendations and a reputation built on results.

The People Behind MARR

Meet the Team

A dedicated team of qualified practitioners, each committed to your skin health and confidence.

Emma Marr

Founder & Lead Practitioner

Emma founded MARR Laser & Skin Clinic with a clear vision: to bring genuinely effective, evidence-based skin and laser treatments to Newry and the surrounding area. Her background in nursing, combined with specialist training in aesthetic medicine and laser therapy, gave her the clinical foundation to build a practice rooted in safety and results. She personally oversees every treatment protocol and trains each member of the team to her own exacting standards.

  • Registered Nurse (NMC)
  • Level 7 Diploma in Aesthetic Medicine
  • Certified Laser Safety Officer
  • 10+ years clinical experience

Sarah Hughes

Senior Skin Therapist

Sarah is a certified dermal therapist specialising in advanced facial treatments, chemical peels and LED therapy. She brings a warm, reassuring presence to every consultation and is passionate about helping clients understand their skin and achieve lasting improvements. Her attention to detail and gentle technique make her a favourite among clients seeking skin rejuvenation treatments.
