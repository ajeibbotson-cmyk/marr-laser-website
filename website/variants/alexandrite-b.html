The Science

What Is Alexandrite Laser?

The Alexandrite laser operates at a wavelength of 755 nanometres, which sits in the optimal absorption range for melanin in the hair follicle. This means the laser energy is absorbed more efficiently by the hair, resulting in more effective follicle destruction with less energy wasted on surrounding tissue.

Originally developed for medical and dermatological applications, the Alexandrite laser has become the gold standard in aesthetic hair removal. Its shorter wavelength compared to diode or Nd:YAG lasers allows for faster repetition rates and larger spot sizes, making treatments quicker and more comfortable.

At MARR Laser & Skin Clinic, we chose Alexandrite technology specifically for its proven clinical track record, patient comfort, and ability to deliver visible results from the very first session.

Our choice of Alexandrite technology is backed by decades of clinical research and regulatory approval.
