Common Questions

Anti-Ageing FAQs

What age should I start anti-ageing treatments?

There is no single right age. Prevention is always easier than correction, so many clients begin in their late twenties or early thirties. However, our treatments are effective at any age and can be tailored to your specific stage of skin ageing.

How soon will I see results?

Many clients notice an immediate improvement in skin tone and tightness after their first session. Collagen remodelling continues for 4-12 weeks after treatment, so results develop progressively and improve with each subsequent session.

Is there any downtime?

LED light therapy and CACI have no downtime whatsoever. Focus Dual RF may cause mild redness for a few hours. All our anti-ageing treatments are designed to fit into your normal routine with minimal disruption.

How many sessions will I need?

A typical course is 6-10 sessions depending on the treatment type and your goals. CACI is most effective with an initial course of 10 sessions, while Focus Dual RF typically requires 3-6 sessions. Your practitioner will recommend the optimal plan during your consultation.

Can I combine anti-ageing treatments?

Absolutely. In fact, combining treatments often delivers the best results. For example, Focus Dual RF for deep collagen stimulation paired with LED therapy for accelerated healing, or CACI for muscle toning combined with RF for skin tightening. Your practitioner will design the ideal combination for you.

Are these treatments suitable for sensitive skin?

Yes. All our anti-ageing treatments can be adjusted in intensity to suit sensitive skin. LED light therapy is particularly gentle and is often used to calm and heal reactive skin. During your consultation, we assess your skin sensitivity and tailor the treatment accordingly.
