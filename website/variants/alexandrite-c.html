Our Technology

Why We Choose Alexandrite

The 755nm wavelength delivers unmatched precision for permanent hair reduction, offering faster treatment times and superior results across a wide range of skin types.

The Science

What Makes Alexandrite the Gold Standard

The Alexandrite laser operates at a wavelength of 755 nanometres, sitting in the optimal absorption range for melanin. At MARR, we chose this technology for its proven clinical track record, patient comfort, and visible results from the very first session.

1

Wavelength Precision

The 755nm wavelength sits in the optimal absorption range for melanin in the hair follicle. This means the laser energy is absorbed more efficiently by the hair, resulting in more effective follicle destruction with less energy wasted on surrounding tissue.

2

Speed

Faster repetition rates and larger spot sizes make treatments quicker and more efficient. Full legs can be treated in under 30 minutes — significantly faster than alternative laser technologies or IPL devices.

3

Safety

Originally developed for medical and dermatological applications, the Alexandrite laser has decades of clinical evidence supporting its safety profile. Our system holds CE medical device certification and meets all UK regulatory standards.

4

Comfort

A built-in cooling system numbs the skin as the laser fires, making treatments far more comfortable than waxing. The shorter pulse duration minimises discomfort significantly compared to other laser types.

5

Results

Up to 90% permanent hair reduction, with visible results after just 2 to 3 treatments. Typically 6 to 8 sessions achieves optimal results — fewer than IPL or diode alternatives require for comparable outcomes.
