About MARR

The people, the passion and the purpose behind Newry's leading skin and laser clinic.

The Founder

Meet Emma

Emma Marr founded MARR Laser & Skin Clinic with a clear vision: to bring genuinely effective, evidence-based skin and laser treatments to Newry and the surrounding area. Her background in nursing, combined with specialist training in aesthetic medicine and laser therapy, gave her the clinical foundation to build a practice rooted in safety and results.

What started as a small clinic driven by one practitioner's dedication to skin health has grown into a trusted destination for clients across Northern Ireland. Emma personally oversees every treatment protocol and trains each member of the team to the same exacting standards she sets for herself.

"Everyone deserves to feel confident in their own skin."

Her philosophy is simple: listen first, advise honestly, and deliver results that speak for themselves. That belief has shaped every aspect of MARR, from the treatments offered to the way clients are cared for.
