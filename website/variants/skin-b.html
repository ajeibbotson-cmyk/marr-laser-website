Common Questions

Skin Treatment FAQs

Which skin treatment is right for me?

Every skin is unique, which is why we begin with a thorough consultation. We assess your skin type, concerns, and goals to recommend the most effective treatment plan tailored specifically to you.

How many sessions will I need?

This depends on the treatment type and your skin concerns. IPL typically requires 3-6 sessions, CO2 resurfacing may need 1-3 sessions, and thread vein treatments usually take 2-4 sessions. Your practitioner will advise during your consultation.

Is there any downtime?

Downtime varies by treatment. IPL and thread vein removal have minimal downtime with possible mild redness for 24-48 hours. CO2 resurfacing involves 5-7 days of healing. Skin tag removal requires no downtime at all. We provide detailed aftercare for each treatment.

Can I combine treatments?

Yes, many clients benefit from a combination approach. For example, IPL for pigmentation alongside CO2 for texture. Your practitioner will design a treatment plan that safely combines the most effective options for your concerns.

Are the treatments safe for all skin types?

Most of our treatments are suitable for a wide range of skin types, though specific settings and approaches are adjusted for your individual needs. During your consultation, we assess your Fitzpatrick skin type and any contraindications to ensure safe, effective treatment.

What results can I expect?

Results depend on the treatment and your starting point. Many clients notice improvement after a single session, with optimal results developing over a course of treatments. We take clinical photographs at each visit so you can clearly track your progress.
