Our Technology

Alexandrite Laser

The Alexandrite 755nm laser is widely recognised as the gold standard for laser hair removal. Its longer wavelength targets the melanin in hair follicles with exceptional precision, delivering faster treatment times, fewer sessions and superior results compared to IPL or diode alternatives.

At MARR, we invested in this technology because we believe our clients deserve the most effective treatment available — not the cheapest option on the market.
