About the Treatment

What Is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal works by directing concentrated light energy into the hair follicle. The pigment (melanin) within the hair absorbs this light, which converts to heat and damages the follicle, inhibiting future growth. Because hair grows in cycles, multiple sessions are required to target follicles during their active growth phase, resulting in progressive and lasting reduction over a course of treatments.

Our clinical-grade equipment is suitable for a wide range of skin types and hair colours. With advanced cooling technology and precision optics, treatments are both comfortable and highly effective, delivering results that simply are not achievable with at-home devices.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients achieve optimal results in 6-8 sessions, spaced 4-6 weeks apart. The exact number depends on the treatment area, hair type, and individual response. Your practitioner will recommend a personalised plan during your consultation.

Does laser hair removal hurt?

Most clients describe a mild snapping sensation, similar to a rubber band. Our advanced cooling system significantly reduces discomfort. Sensitive areas may feel slightly more intense, but the sensation is brief and very manageable.

How should I prepare for my session?

Shave the treatment area 24 hours before your appointment. Avoid sun exposure, tanning beds, and self-tan for 2 weeks prior. Do not wax or pluck for 4 weeks before treatment, as the hair follicle must be intact for the laser to be effective.

Are there any side effects?

Temporary redness and mild swelling are normal and typically resolve within a few hours. We provide full aftercare instructions to ensure the best recovery and minimise any discomfort following your session.

Is laser hair removal permanent?

Laser hair removal provides permanent hair reduction. Most clients experience 80-90% reduction after completing their course. Occasional maintenance sessions may be needed to address any regrowth from dormant follicles.

What skin types can be treated?

Our advanced diode laser technology is effective across a wide range of skin types. During your free consultation, we will assess your skin and hair type to confirm suitability and recommend the most effective treatment parameters for you.
