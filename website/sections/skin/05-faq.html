Skin Treatment FAQs

Which skin treatment is right for me?

Every skin is unique, which is why we begin with a thorough consultation. We assess your skin type, concerns, and goals to recommend the most effective treatment plan.

How often should I have treatments?

This depends on the treatment type. Clinical facials can be monthly, chemical peels every 4–6 weeks, and rejuvenation treatments are typically spaced 4–6 weeks apart for a course of 3–6 sessions.

Is there any downtime?

Clinical facials have no downtime. Chemical peels may involve 2–5 days of light peeling. Rejuvenation treatments may cause redness for 24–48 hours. We provide detailed aftercare for each treatment.

Can I combine treatments?

Yes, many of our clients benefit from a combination approach. We often pair clinical facials with peels or rejuvenation treatments for enhanced results. Your practitioner will advise on the best combination.

Are the products medical-grade?

Yes, we exclusively use professional-grade, clinically-proven products. These are significantly more effective than over-the-counter alternatives and are only available through trained practitioners.
