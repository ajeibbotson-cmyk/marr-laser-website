What Is Laser Hair Removal?

Laser hair removal works by directing concentrated light energy into the hair follicle. The pigment (melanin) within the hair absorbs this light, which converts to heat and damages the follicle, inhibiting future growth. Because hair grows in cycles, multiple sessions are required to target follicles during their active growth phase, resulting in progressive and lasting reduction over a course of treatments.

Our clinical-grade equipment is suitable for a wide range of skin types and hair colours. With advanced cooling technology and precision optics, treatments are both comfortable and highly effective, delivering results that simply are not achievable with at-home devices.

  • Suitable for most skin types
