Who Is a Good Candidate?

Laser hair removal is effective for a broad range of people, though results depend on individual factors such as hair colour, skin type, and hormonal profile. A free consultation allows us to assess your suitability and design a personalised treatment plan.

Ideal for:

  • Dark hair on lighter skin
  • Those seeking permanent reduction
  • People tired of shaving or waxing
  • Anyone wanting smoother skin

Good to know:

  • Multiple sessions needed
  • Avoid sun exposure before treatment
  • Results vary by individual
  • Free consultation to assess suitability
