About the Clinic

We Add Confidence to Your Skin and Life to Your Years

Marr Laser & Skin Clinic brings together medical expertise and advanced aesthetic technology in a setting designed around you. Based in Newry, Co. Down, we offer a considered range of laser, skin and wellness treatments grounded in clinical evidence and delivered with genuine care.

Every treatment plan begins with a thorough consultation, because lasting results start with understanding your skin, your goals and what matters most to you. Our approach is never one-size-fits-all. We combine precision diagnostics with personalised protocols so that every visit moves you closer to the outcome you want.
