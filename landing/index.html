Frequently Asked Questions

Is laser hair removal painful?
Most clients describe the sensation as a warm rubber band snap. Our advanced cooling technology minimises discomfort throughout the treatment.
How many sessions will I need?
Most clients require 6‚Äì8 sessions spaced 4‚Äì6 weeks apart for optimal results. The number depends on hair type, skin colour, and treatment area.
What skin types can be treated?
Our clinic uses multiple laser wavelengths (755nm, 808nm, 1064nm) to safely treat all skin types from Fitzpatrick I to VI.

There is no downtime ‚Äî you can return to normal activities immediately. Some mild redness may occur for 1‚Äì2 hours post-treatment.
