Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 3-in-1 treatment work?

Our treatment combines three advanced technologies: Radiesse provides immediate structural support and volumization, Exosomes activate cellular renewal and repair, while Salmon DNA delivers deep hydration and skin quality improvement. These components work synergistically to provide comprehensive facial rejuvenation.

Is the treatment painful?

Most clients report minimal discomfort during the procedure. We use topical numbing cream and the treatments are performed by expert practitioners who prioritize your comfort. Some clients may experience mild tenderness for 1-2 days after treatment.

When will I see results?

You'll notice immediate improvement in facial contours and volume after the treatment. The results continue to improve over the following 2-3 months as the regenerative effects of Exosomes and Salmon DNA enhance your skin quality and stimulate collagen production.

How long do results last?

Results typically last 18-24 months, though this can vary depending on individual factors such as age, lifestyle, and skin condition. Many clients choose to have maintenance treatments every 18-24 months to maintain optimal results.

Is there any downtime?

There's minimal downtime with our 3-in-1 treatment. Most clients return to their normal activities immediately. You may experience mild swelling or tenderness for 1-3 days, which can easily be covered with makeup if needed.

How many sessions do I need?

Most clients achieve their desired results with just one session. However, depending on your individual goals and the degree of aging, some clients may benefit from a second session after 6-12 months.
