Laser Hair Removal

Our medical-grade laser technology delivers safe, effective, and long-lasting hair removal for all skin types. Using the latest diode and Nd:YAG systems, we target hair at the follicle for smooth, hair-free skin with minimal discomfort. Each session is tailored to your skin tone, hair type, and the area being treated.

From £45 per session

How many sessions will I need?

Most clients see optimal results after 6–8 sessions, spaced 4–6 weeks apart. Hair grows in cycles, and multiple sessions ensure we target follicles during their active growth phase. During your consultation, we will provide a personalised treatment schedule based on the area and your hair type.

Is laser hair removal painful?

Most clients describe the sensation as a mild snap or warm pinch, similar to a rubber band lightly flicking the skin. Our advanced cooling systems significantly reduce discomfort, and most sessions are well tolerated. Sensitivity varies by area, and we always adjust settings to keep you comfortable.

Is it suitable for all skin types?

Yes. Our clinic uses dual-wavelength technology that is effective and safe across all skin tones, from very fair to deeply pigmented skin. During your consultation, we perform a patch test and select the appropriate settings for your individual skin profile.
