What to Expect

Your Treatment Timeline

1

Session 1-2

Initial treatment targeting active hair follicles. You may notice shedding within 1-2 weeks.

2

Session 3-4

Significant reduction becomes visible. Treatment intervals may be adjusted based on progress.

3

Session 5-6

Majority of hair growth eliminated. Fine-tuning remaining areas for optimal results.

4

Maintenance

Occasional top-up sessions to maintain smooth, hair-free results long term.

Results vary by individual. Your practitioner will advise on the optimal treatment schedule during your consultation.
